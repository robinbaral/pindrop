Pindrop Privacy

TLDR: We collect nothing!

Pindrop does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data of any kind.

All game data (player names, scores, and session history) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, and no advertising.

What we collect: Nothing.

Third parties:
Pindrop does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children:
Pindrop is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data whatsoever, there are no special considerations regarding children's privacy.

Contact:
If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at pindropapp@proton.me.
